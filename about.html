About us

Welcome
To Our Gym

Our Grand gym has been open for quite some years now; and it has to be 5 years. The 5 years we have been providing our services have been the very successful ones. We started providing our services because we want to help those people who want to make their bodies very strong, burn a lot of fats in a short period, and improve the way their bodies function and respond to different activities.

Our mission as a gym is to make sure to decrease health cases that arise due to physical weakness of the body, and improve the way our bodies function and respond to different activities like decision making; that is mentally.

We were inspired by our fellow teams who also provide gym services. We saw how people were doing their exercises, and later on noted that the exercises were having a very positive result on how their bodies usually responded. With this, we decided to help our collegues train with other people so that their bodies should also improve its functionality and its response to different situations.

In a nutshell, we, the Grand Gym looks forward to training with you to be very fit and improve your bodies' functionality and its response to different situations.

Our
Success Stories

As a gym, we have gone through quite a lot of difficulties and successes.

To begin with, we only started with one class and that was the High Intensity Interval Training class with 50 people. We trained with them everyday and we were happy to see our aim be accomplished.

As time passed, we were sponsored and then, we added two classes namely Abs class and Indoor cycling or spinning. We had over 200 people coming to do classes with us and as expected, they were really helped. Afterwards, we added the boxing class not so long ago and its also reaching to our expectations.

With our whole team as a gym, it is our hope and expectation that you will also join us very soon.

Let's visit the gym everyday and attend different classes so that there should be changes to our bodies; they should be physically fit.
