1. HIIT CLASS

High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is type of workout that makes one to give all his or her strength in a very short period of time and it is followed by short recovery periods. The training sessions usually follow a circuit taking in a range of bodyweight, light-weight and cardio exercises.

We help people in these classes to become stronger, burn much fats in a short period of time, improve one's agility and speed and to help their hearts to be in functional properly.

2. ABS CLASS

Exercises in this particular class are a great way of building core strength and core stability. The classes are short and painful as it takes in a wide range of core exercises.

These classes help one to build a strong core that is located right in the centre of the body and a lot of movement for a range of sports starts in that core; thus preventing injuries that can come about due to having a weak core. They also help one to improve posture and back pains since one has a strong core that is essential support for the back.

3. INDOOR CYCLING CLASS

This class is sometimes called the spinning class. In this class, the exercises differ from each other; other people focus on their speed and others focus on their endurance.

These exercises help to increase cardiovascular fitness, lose weight as one single spin results into burning of 700 calories, and they also help to strengthen the legs and tightens the backside.
